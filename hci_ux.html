üìå Project Overview

This research explores how digital tools can improve the experience of international students moving to London by creating a central information hub

  • Accommodation support
  • Financial tips and student discounts
  • Language and university adaptation guidance
  • Social networking and student community engagement
  • Mental health and wellbeing support

This project was completed within a ten week HCI module Our goal was to identify real pain points and co design a simple and inclusive solution that helps students adapt connect and thrive

üéØ Project Goals

Newly arrived students face fragmented info across housing finance transport wellbeing and social life We aimed to co design a central hub that reduces effort improves clarity and supports faster adaptation

  • Understand student pain points through interviews and analysis
  • Develop a user centric solution using design thinking empathize define ideate prototype test
  • Prototype a supportive application that unifies housing finance mental health and social features
