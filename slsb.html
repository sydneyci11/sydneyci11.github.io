Seneca Lake Scenic Byway: SEO & Content Strategy Analysis

Consulting project for a regional tourism organization (Oct–Dec 2024), focused on improving digital visibility through analytics and SEO optimization.

🔍 Project Context & Goals

As part of a strategic partnership, we worked to enhance the web presence of the Seneca Lake Scenic Byway. Our goal was to increase discoverability, drive organic traffic, and align content with high-interest seasonal queries.

📊 Analytics-Driven Discovery

We used Google Analytics 4 and Search Console to evaluate site performance. Issues included:

  • High bounce rate on key landing pages
  • Low CTR from search results
  • Missed opportunities in high-volume keyword targeting

🛠️ SEO Implementation

Implemented changes in the Squarespace CMS including:

  • Meta description refinement and page title optimization
  • Keyword-rich content restructuring
  • Focused targeting of phrases like "Seneca Lake wineries", "snowmobiling NY"

🚀 Results

Within a short 2-month window, we saw promising early results:

  • CTR increased by 19.67%
  • Top-10 keyword visibility grew: 477 → 489
  • 200%+ traffic growth on high-season pages (e.g., Snowmobiling, Ice Skating)

Although long-term SEO gains require more time to stabilize, our early impact validated the strategy.
