🐾 AHDC Diagnostic Price Sensitivity Analysis

Collaborated with Cornell’s Animal Health Diagnostic Center to analyze test demand data from 2019–2023. Built dynamic Tableau dashboards to visualize these trends and assist strategic pricing decisions.
